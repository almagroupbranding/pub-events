Venue fit

Clear expectations before anyone emails

This page sets the tone before the enquiry arrives. It saves staff time, improves lead quality, and positions the function room as a proper private-hire space rather than “just a room above a pub”.

Events we welcome

Adult celebrations, dining-led gatherings, wakes, business events, private meals and well-organised local groups.

Events we may decline

18th birthdays, children’s parties, mostly under-21 events, or loud late-night parties that do not suit the pub.

Approval process

The owner reviews the details, confirms availability, then offers a hold, deposit request or alternative date.
